About Our Organisation

Welcome to Devyaan Foundation, a charitable organization committed to social welfare, environmental preservation, and animal protection. Our mission is to uplift underprivileged communities, spread awareness about crucial social and environmental issues, and create a compassionate society where every individual and living being is treated with dignity and respect.

At Devyaan Foundation, we believe that small actions lead to big changes. Through our various initiatives, we strive to provide essential services, education, and support to those in need while promoting sustainable living and humane treatment of animals.

Our Objectives

  1. Support for Vulnerable Populations:

    We are committed to providing essential resources and services to tribal communities, economically disadvantaged individuals, women, persons with disabilities, the elderly, children, and other marginalized groups. Our initiatives include:

    • Food and Clothing: Distributing nutritious meals and clothing to those in need.
    • Education and Training: Offering educational programs and vocational training to empower individuals.
    • Housing and Healthcare: Facilitating access to safe housing and healthcare services, including ambulance support and medical assistance.
  2. Awareness Campaigns:

    We are committed to organizing various awareness campaigns to educate and inform the public, including:

    • Campaigns on Public Health: Disease prevention in collaboration with organizations like WHO and NABARD.
    • Charity Events: Raising funds and awareness for critical issues.
    • Community Initiatives: Mass marriage programs that promote social welfare and unity.
  3. Establishment of Health and Education Facilities:

    Our NGO aims to address community needs by establishing and operating essential facilities:

    • Hospitals and Health Services: Establishing hospitals, clinics, and de-addiction centers to promote health and well-being.
    • Educational Institutions: Setting up schools and colleges to provide quality education and foster learning.
  4. Animal Welfare Advocacy:

    Recognizing the importance of compassion towards animals, we promote animal welfare through various initiatives:

    • Shelter Construction: Building and maintaining shelters for stray animals and providing necessary veterinary services.
    • Pet Adoption Awareness: Raising awareness about the benefits of pet adoption and responsible pet ownership.
    • Community Engagement: Collaborating with other organizations to create a pet-friendly culture through education and outreach programs.
  5. Our Approach:

    We follow a community-first, impact-driven methodology to ensure our efforts bring meaningful and lasting change. Our approach includes:

    • Community-Centered: We believe in working with communities, not for them.
    • Sustainable Solutions: Our programs are designed for long-term impact.
    • Evidence-Based: We use data and research to guide our interventions.
    • Collaborative: We partner with local organizations and stakeholders.

Organisation Structure

At Devyaan Foundation, our organizational structure ensures efficient management, transparency, and impactful program delivery. Guided by a committed Board of Trustees, we operate through dedicated departments focused on social welfare, education, healthcare, environmental conservation, and animal welfare.

Each department is led by experienced professionals, volunteers, and community leaders who collaborate to design, implement, and oversee initiatives. We prioritize grassroots engagement and participatory decision-making to ensure our work addresses real community needs.

With a strong network of volunteers, donors, and partner organizations, we maintain a structured yet flexible approach—driving sustainable change and supporting communities in need.

Our Vision

Vision Statement

At Devyaan Foundation, we envision a world where everyone has equal access to basic needs, education, healthcare, and justice—ensuring dignity and opportunity for all. We strive for a society rooted in compassion, sustainability, and inclusivity.

Our vision includes empowering the underprivileged, preserving the environment, and promoting animal welfare. Through long-term development, awareness programs, and community-driven efforts, we aim to create a just, self-reliant, and harmonious world for future generations.

Our Mission

Mission Statement

Our mission is to uplift underprivileged communities by ensuring access to food, shelter, education, healthcare, and legal support—promoting a life of dignity for all.

We are committed to social welfare, environmental conservation, and animal protection through impactful initiatives and awareness campaigns. Our focus goes beyond relief—we aim for long-term transformation and inclusive development that empowers individuals and builds a better future.
